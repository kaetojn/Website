BUYING

Buying with us

We are here to help fulfil your real estate needs. For most people, purchasing a property is one of the most important financial commitments they would make, and we are here to ease that process for you. We will work diligently with you to provide useful information that would assist you in making an optimal decision about any property that you intend to purchase

 

Our expertise encompasses all housing types including: single family homes, townhomes, condominiums, lofts and apartments, co-ops, and multi-unit buildings. We welcome first time buyers, local prospects and those moving to Toronto, as well as current property owners wishing to sell their houses, trade up or acquire income-producing properties.

 

While there are tons of real estate sales people out there that just help clients purchase properties without owning one themselves, our approach is advisory and consulting based on both training and experience which we have. We've suceeded in turning our dreams of owning into reality, and are continuing to do that for others as well as ourselves. We welcome you to tap from this experience!

The buying process

Together we will:

  1. Determine your budget and comfort zone;
  2. Itemize all the requirements for your home;
  3. Determine the neighbourhoods of your choice;
  4. View homes that match your criteria as closely as possible;
  5. Do a preliminary computer search to get an idea of suitable homes;
  6. Go over the factors that will affect future resale (after all, we will probably be the ones that sell your home again when you're ready to move!);
  7. Review the mechanical details associated with homes, e.g. wiring, plumbing, roofing, various heating and cooling systems, etc.;
  8. Review the process of entering into an agreement of purchase and sale.

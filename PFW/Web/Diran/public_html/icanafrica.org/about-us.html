About ICANA

ICANA supports African growth via facilitating a network of resourceful individuals/organizations possessing genuine interests in African developmental initiatives.

Through partnerships with NGOs/NPOs/individuals with shared focus, ICANA creates a platform whereby efforts towards social growth and development can be consolidated. ICANA works in cohort with social developmental organizations to expedite programs that positively develop communities within Africa. A phase of the ICANA initiative is to promote the efforts of NGOs and devise avenues via which resources can be channeled, internally, towards actualizing set goals. One of such means is creating a system among NGOs/NPOs whereby each organization can interact directly with networked organizations and seed or map out strategies of unified operation(s) to promote a consensual and established cause. ICANA creates a seamless transition among networked organizations whereby resources (financial, manpower, infrastructural et al) are made available, hereby attempting to reduce the burden of scouting for ‘external’ assistance, alone. Through meticulous research and evaluation, the criteria upon which networked organizations will be selected are guaranteed to ensure a resourceful base with a variety of opportunities to be gained by every cell within the network. ICANA will not be targeting financially established organizations, as this negates the purpose of mutual networking. Rather, ICANA will be supporting young organizations that possess authentic/genuine interest in the African developmental cause. With these young organizations, ICANA appreciates the genuinity and focus towards a common goal. Furthermore, due to the size and structure of these networked organizations, it is appreciated that no one organization is self-sufficient and as such, ICANA will support the growth of these organizations and most importantly, maximize their potential and efforts towards social constructs.

Projects

Heal the world initiative in collaboration with Hands of a Hero Foundation.

ICANA is collaborating with Hands of a Hero in a health initiative focused on the quality of health care in Oboyan, village located in the Eastern Region of Ghana. This initiative aims to garner (by donations & purchases) medical supplies for the community in order to provide access to the basic medical supplies needed – i.e. disposable heat/cold packs, band aids, first aid kits, hand sanitizer, disposable gloves, lab coats, blood pressure cuffs, and thermometers). This would initiate the necessary steps required in greatly improving the health of its residents. In addition, this project will also include a focus on the oral healthcare of the members of the community and would involve bringing dentists to the community to provide mass oral checkups, teach dental hygiene and provide equipment such as toothpaste, toothbrushes, and other items for oral health.

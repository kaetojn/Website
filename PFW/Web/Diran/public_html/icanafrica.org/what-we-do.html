What We Do

ICANA focuses on the following areas of needs within communities:

Education programs

We work with organizations to help meet their goals to provide education assistance in communities that lack the means.

Healthcare programs

We collaborate with organizations to help support communities that lack basic healthcare necessities.

Poverty alleviation

We partner with NGOs to help provide relief to communities that are in dire poverty

Vocation programs

We support organizations that reach out to mentor & train young individuals in life skills

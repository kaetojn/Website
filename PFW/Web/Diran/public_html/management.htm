MANAGEMENT

Management Services

We specialize in identifying, locating, negotiating, and facilitating the search process for both landlords & tenants searching for a good tenant or rental property in the Greater Toronto Area. For owners lacking the time, capability or expertise needed for the day-to-day management of their real estate investments, our property management team can aid with the following services:

Landlords

Marketing of the investment property to obtain maximal income and keep vacancies low
Ensure a thorough process and due diligence in the selection of prospective tenants
Routine maintenance of rental property to ensure it remains in a decent state
Facilitating the accounting process for generated revenue
Advisory on how to get better value for rental income
Help draft, negotiate and ratify rental agreements within the bounds of the law
Facilitate landlord and tenant relationship

Tenants

Assistance in securing a good property to rent within your budget
Advisory on what to do to get a property of interest
Facilitating landlord and tenant relationship
Helping with additional issues regarding tenancy
